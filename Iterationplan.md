## Iteration Plan

The development follows an **Agile methodology** , with the project divided into four primary iterations to ensure incremental delivery and continuous feedback.

| Iteration | Focus | Expected Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Iteration 1** | Requirements and Prototype | Finalized Product Backlog, UI Prototype, and Brief Report. |
| **Iteration 2** | Core TA Functions | Implementation of Profile Creation and CV Upload features. |
| **Iteration 3** | Job Management | Development of the Job Posting and Application system. |
| **Iteration 4** | Advanced Features | Application Tracking, Workload Management, and AI-powered functions. |

---

###  Key Assessment Milestones
* **22nd March 2026**: First Assessment (Product Backlog, Prototype, and Report).
* **12th April 2026**: Intermediate Assessment (Working Software Version 2 Demonstration).
* **24th May 2026**: Final Assessment (Full Software, Video Demo, and Final Report).
