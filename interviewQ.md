## Interview Questions

The following interview questions are prepared for different stakeholders.

### Questions for Teaching Assistants (Applicants)

1. How do you currently find available TA job opportunities?
2. What steps do you usually follow when applying for a TA position?
3. What difficulties do you experience during the application process?
4. How do you currently track the status of your applications?
5. What features would you like to see in an online TA recruitment system?

### Questions for Module Organisers (MOs)

1. How do you currently advertise or post TA positions?
2. How do you review and select applicants?
3. What problems do you face when managing applications?
4. What information about applicants is most important when making selections?
5. What features would help you manage TA recruitment more efficiently?

### Questions for Administrators

1. How do you currently monitor the workload of Teaching Assistants?
2. What challenges do you face when coordinating TA assignments?
3. What information would you like to see in a TA management system?
4. How important is it to track TA workload across different modules?
5. What administrative features should be included in the system?
