**Date:** 2026.3.16

**1. User Interview Summary**

Today, a user interview was conducted about the TA recruitment process.

**2. Recruitment Information Source and Application Process**

The interviewee said that they usually learn about TA job opportunities through friends. The application process generally includes joining a group chat, filling in a questionnaire sent by the teacher, submitting a CV, and then waiting for the teacher to notify the result.

**3. Application Progress**

Regarding application progress, the interviewee mentioned that they mainly learn the result through email. If they do not receive any notification, they usually assume that they have not been selected. This shows that the current application status is not transparent enough.

**4. Expected System Features**

For a future online TA recruitment system, the interviewee believed that the most important feature would be to provide clear and detailed information about the course and the position, so that applicants can understand the requirements in advance.
